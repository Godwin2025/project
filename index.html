Healthfirst Mobile App Features

Video Consultations

Connect with healthfirst providers through secure video calls. Book appointments, get prescriptions, and receive medical advice without leaving home.

Appointment Booking

Schedule in-person or virtual appointments with specialists. View available time slots, choose your preferred doctor, and receive reminders.

Health Tracking

Monitor vital signs, medication adherence, exercise, and other health metrics. View trends and share data with healthfirst providers.

Prescription Management

View current medications, refill prescriptions, and receive medication reminders to stay on track with your treatment plan.

Technical Information

This mobile healthfirst application is built with React Native, allowing it to run on both iOS and Android devices from a single codebase. It leverages various libraries including React Navigation for routing, React Native WebRTC for video calls, and React Native Charts for health visualization.

The app demonstrates best practices in mobile healthcare application development, with a focus on usability, accessibility, and security.
